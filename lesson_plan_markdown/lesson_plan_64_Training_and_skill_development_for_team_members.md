# Building a Wardley Mapping Culture: Training and Skill Development for Startup Teams

**Duration:** 6 hours (full-day workshop)
**Target Audience:** Startup founders, executives, and team leaders across various functions

## Learning Objectives

| Objective | Bloom's Taxonomy Level |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| Analyze the core principles and components of Wardley Mapping | Analyzing |
| Create basic Wardley Maps for familiar systems within their startup | Creating |
| Evaluate the strategic implications of Wardley Maps for decision-making in startups | Evaluating |
| Design a plan for implementing Wardley Mapping training within their startup | Creating |
| Synthesize Wardley Mapping with other strategic tools used in startups | Synthesizing |

## Key Concepts
* Wardley Mapping
* Value chains
* Evolution axis
* Component mapping
* Strategic decision-making
* Continuous learning culture
* Cross-functional integration
* Scenario planning
* Multi-map analysis

## Prior Knowledge
* Basic understanding of startup operations and strategy
* Familiarity with common business analysis tools (e.g., SWOT, Business Model Canvas)

## Materials Needed
* Laptops or tablets with internet access
* Digital whiteboard software (e.g., Miro, Mural)
* Wardley Mapping software or templates
* Printed handouts of sample Wardley Maps
* Post-it notes and markers for physical activities

## Lesson Structure
### Engage
**Duration:** 30 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present a real-world startup case study highlighting strategic challenges. Facilitate a discussion on current approaches to strategic decision-making.

**Learner Activities:** Participate in group discussion, sharing experiences and challenges in strategic planning within their startups.

**Resources Used:** Case study presentation, discussion prompts

**Differentiation:** Provide multiple case studies catering to different startup stages and industries

**Technology Integration:** Use interactive polling software to gather and display participant responses

### Explore
**Duration:** 90 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Introduce core concepts of Wardley Mapping through interactive presentation. Guide participants through a simple mapping exercise.

**Learner Activities:** Create a basic Wardley Map for a familiar system within their startup. Share and discuss maps in small groups.

**Resources Used:** Wardley Mapping software, sample maps, guided exercise instructions

**Differentiation:** Provide additional support for novice mappers, challenge experienced participants with more complex scenarios

**Technology Integration:** Use collaborative mapping software for real-time creation and sharing of maps

### Explain
**Duration:** 60 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Elaborate on advanced Wardley Mapping concepts and techniques. Demonstrate multi-map analysis and scenario planning.

**Learner Activities:** Analyze sample multi-maps and discuss potential strategic implications. Practice scenario planning using Wardley Maps.

**Resources Used:** Advanced Wardley Map examples, scenario planning templates

**Differentiation:** Offer optional deep-dive sessions on specific advanced topics

**Technology Integration:** Utilize interactive simulations to demonstrate the impact of different scenarios on maps

### Elaborate
**Duration:** 120 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Facilitate a collaborative mapping session focused on a common startup challenge. Guide discussions on integrating Wardley Mapping with other strategic tools.

**Learner Activities:** Work in cross-functional teams to create comprehensive Wardley Maps for their startups. Present and critique maps, discussing strategic insights.

**Resources Used:** Digital collaboration tools, presentation software

**Differentiation:** Assign roles within teams based on individual strengths and experience levels

**Technology Integration:** Use breakout rooms in virtual settings for team collaboration, digital presentation tools for map sharing

### Evaluate
**Duration:** 60 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Guide participants in creating action plans for implementing Wardley Mapping in their startups. Facilitate peer feedback and discussion.

**Learner Activities:** Develop and present a plan for building a Wardley Mapping culture in their startup. Provide constructive feedback on peers' plans.

**Resources Used:** Action plan templates, peer evaluation rubrics

**Differentiation:** Offer individualized coaching sessions for participants needing additional support

**Technology Integration:** Use project management tools to create and share implementation plans

## Assessment Methods
* **Formative**: Ongoing observation and feedback during mapping exercises and discussions
  - Alignment: Assesses participants' ability to apply Wardley Mapping concepts in real-time
* **Summative**: Evaluation of final Wardley Maps and implementation plans
  - Alignment: Measures participants' ability to create comprehensive maps and strategize implementation in their specific startup contexts
* **Peer Assessment**: Structured peer feedback on presented implementation plans
  - Alignment: Encourages critical thinking and application of learned concepts to diverse startup scenarios

## Differentiation Strategies
* **Novice Wardley Mappers**: Provide additional guided exercises, offer one-on-one support, use simplified mapping templates
* **Experienced Strategic Planners**: Offer advanced scenario planning challenges, encourage integration with existing strategic frameworks, facilitate discussions on complex multi-map analysis
* **Technical vs. Non-Technical Participants**: Provide industry-specific mapping examples, adjust complexity of technology components in mapping exercises

## Cross-Disciplinary Connections
* Linking Wardley Mapping to financial forecasting and budgeting
* Integrating Wardley Mapping with product development and innovation processes
* Applying Wardley Mapping to human resource planning and team structure optimization

## Real-World Applications
* Using Wardley Maps to identify potential disruptors in the startup's market
* Applying mapping techniques to optimize supply chains and partnerships
* Leveraging Wardley Mapping for more effective pitch presentations to investors

## Metacognition Opportunities
* Reflection sessions on how Wardley Mapping challenges or reinforces existing mental models of business strategy
* Journaling exercises on personal insights gained from the mapping process
* Group discussions on how Wardley Mapping might change decision-making processes within their startups

## Extension Activities
* Conducting a Wardley Mapping workshop with their own startup team
* Creating a series of evolving maps to track strategic changes over a 3-6 month period
* Participating in online Wardley Mapping communities and contributing to open-source mapping projects

## Safety Considerations
* Ensure confidentiality agreements are in place when sharing sensitive startup information during exercises
* Discuss ethical considerations in strategic planning and competitive analysis

## Reflection Questions
### For Learners
* How has Wardley Mapping changed your perspective on your startup's strategic positioning?
* What challenges do you anticipate in implementing Wardley Mapping in your organization, and how might you overcome them?
* How can you integrate Wardley Mapping with other strategic tools you currently use?

### For Facilitator
* How effectively did the workshop cater to the diverse needs and experience levels of participants?
* What aspects of Wardley Mapping seemed to resonate most with the startup context, and how can this be emphasized in future sessions?
* How can the workshop be improved to better facilitate the transfer of Wardley Mapping skills to participants' real-world startup environments?

## Adaptations for Virtual Learning
* Use virtual breakout rooms for small group discussions and exercises
* Employ digital whiteboarding tools for collaborative mapping sessions
* Incorporate more frequent breaks and varied activities to maintain engagement in online settings
* Utilize asynchronous pre-work and post-session assignments to maximize live session productivity
