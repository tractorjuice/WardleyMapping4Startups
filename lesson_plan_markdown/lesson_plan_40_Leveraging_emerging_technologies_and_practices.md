# Leveraging Emerging Technologies and Practices with Wardley Mapping

**Duration:** 4 hours
**Target Audience:** Professional learners in government and public sector startups

## Learning Objectives

| Objective | Bloom's Taxonomy Level |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| Analyze the potential impact of emerging technologies on public sector value chains using Wardley Mapping | Analyzing |
| Develop strategies for incorporating emerging technologies into public sector innovation initiatives | Creating |
| Evaluate the risks and opportunities associated with emerging technologies in the context of government services | Evaluating |
| Design a Wardley Map that incorporates emerging technologies for a specific public sector challenge | Creating |

## Key Concepts
* Wardley Mapping
* Emerging technologies
* Disruptive innovation
* Public sector innovation
* Value chain analysis
* Technology evolution
* Risk assessment
* Strategic planning

## Prior Knowledge
* Basic understanding of Wardley Mapping concepts
* Familiarity with public sector operations and challenges
* General awareness of current technological trends

## Materials Needed
* Computers with internet access
* Wardley Mapping software or large paper sheets and markers
* Case study materials on public sector challenges
* Access to online resources on emerging technologies

## Lesson Structure
### Engage
**Duration:** 30 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present a brief overview of a recent emerging technology that has impacted government services. Facilitate a discussion on its potential implications.

**Learner Activities:** Participate in a group discussion, sharing experiences and initial thoughts on the impact of emerging technologies in their work.

**Resources Used:** Recent news article or case study on emerging technology in government

**Differentiation:** Provide multiple examples to cater to different areas of public sector work

**Technology Integration:** Use an interactive polling tool to gather initial thoughts and display results in real-time

### Explore
**Duration:** 60 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Guide learners through the process of identifying relevant emerging technologies for their specific public sector contexts. Introduce the concept of mapping these technologies on a Wardley Map.

**Learner Activities:** Research emerging technologies relevant to their work. Begin drafting a basic Wardley Map incorporating these technologies.

**Resources Used:** Online resources on emerging technologies, Wardley Mapping software or templates

**Differentiation:** Provide curated lists of technologies for those less familiar with current trends

**Technology Integration:** Use collaborative online whiteboards for group mapping exercises

### Explain
**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present detailed explanations of how to analyze emerging technologies using Wardley Mapping, including evolution stages and interdependencies.

**Learner Activities:** Take notes, ask questions, and begin to refine their initial maps based on the new information.

**Resources Used:** Presentation slides, example Wardley Maps showing technology evolution

**Differentiation:** Provide additional one-on-one support for those struggling with the concepts

**Technology Integration:** Use screen sharing to demonstrate live Wardley Mapping techniques

### Elaborate
**Duration:** 90 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present a complex public sector case study. Guide learners through the process of creating a comprehensive Wardley Map that incorporates emerging technologies to address the challenge.

**Learner Activities:** Work in small groups to develop a detailed Wardley Map for the case study, incorporating emerging technologies and strategies for leveraging them.

**Resources Used:** Case study materials, Wardley Mapping software or large paper sheets

**Differentiation:** Assign roles within groups based on individual strengths and experiences

**Technology Integration:** Use breakout rooms for virtual group work, with shared digital workspaces

### Evaluate
**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Facilitate group presentations of Wardley Maps. Lead a discussion on the strategies proposed and their potential impact.

**Learner Activities:** Present group Wardley Maps, explain strategies for leveraging emerging technologies, and provide constructive feedback to other groups.

**Resources Used:** Presentation tools, evaluation rubric

**Differentiation:** Allow for various presentation formats (e.g., verbal, visual, written)

**Technology Integration:** Use virtual presentation tools with annotation features for feedback

## Assessment Methods
* **Formative**: Ongoing observation and feedback during group work and discussions
  - Alignment: Assesses ability to apply Wardley Mapping concepts to real-world scenarios
* **Summative**: Evaluation of final group Wardley Maps and proposed strategies
  - Alignment: Assesses ability to create comprehensive strategies for leveraging emerging technologies in public sector contexts

## Differentiation Strategies
* **Novice professionals**: Provide additional resources on Wardley Mapping basics, offer more structured templates for mapping exercises
* **Experienced professionals**: Encourage exploration of more complex scenarios, facilitate peer mentoring opportunities
