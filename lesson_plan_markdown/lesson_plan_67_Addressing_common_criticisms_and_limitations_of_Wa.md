# Addressing Criticisms and Limitations of Wardley Mapping for Startups

**Duration:** 3 hours
**Target Audience:** Startup founders, business strategists, and innovation managers

## Learning Objectives

| Objective | Bloom's Taxonomy Level |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| Analyze common criticisms and limitations of Wardley Mapping in the startup context | Analyzing |
| Evaluate potential solutions to overcome Wardley Mapping challenges | Evaluating |
| Create strategies to enhance the application of Wardley Mapping for startup innovation | Creating |

## Key Concepts
* Wardley Mapping
* Strategic planning
* Innovation management
* Complexity in startups
* Subjectivity in mapping
* Disruptive innovation
* Value chain analysis
* Collaborative strategy

## Prior Knowledge
* Basic understanding of Wardley Mapping principles
* Familiarity with startup ecosystems and challenges
* General knowledge of strategic planning concepts

## Materials Needed
* Whiteboard or digital collaboration tool
* Wardley Mapping templates
* Case studies of startup strategies
* Access to online Wardley Mapping resources

## Lesson Structure
### Engage
**Duration:** 30 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present a startup scenario with complex strategic challenges. Introduce the concept of Wardley Mapping and its potential limitations.

**Learner Activities:** Brainstorm potential uses and challenges of applying Wardley Mapping to the given scenario.

**Resources Used:** Startup case study, Wardley Mapping overview slides

**Differentiation:** Provide additional context for those less familiar with startups or Wardley Mapping

**Technology Integration:** Use digital whiteboard for collaborative brainstorming

### Explore
**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Guide learners through a detailed examination of common Wardley Mapping criticisms and limitations.

**Learner Activities:** In small groups, analyze specific criticisms (complexity, subjectivity, focus on existing value chains) and their impact on startups.

**Resources Used:** Criticism analysis worksheet, expert quotes from the content

**Differentiation:** Assign different criticisms to groups based on their expertise

**Technology Integration:** Use breakout rooms for virtual group discussions

### Explain
**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Present potential solutions and enhancements to Wardley Mapping for startups. Facilitate a discussion on their effectiveness.

**Learner Activities:** Participate in a facilitated discussion, sharing insights from the exploration phase and relating them to presented solutions.

**Resources Used:** Presentation on Wardley Mapping enhancements, discussion prompts

**Differentiation:** Encourage contributions from diverse professional backgrounds

**Technology Integration:** Use interactive polling to gauge opinions on proposed solutions

### Elaborate
**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Introduce a challenge for learners to apply enhanced Wardley Mapping techniques to a startup scenario.

**Learner Activities:** In pairs, create an enhanced Wardley Map for a given startup scenario, incorporating solutions to common criticisms.

**Resources Used:** Enhanced Wardley Mapping templates, startup scenario descriptions

**Differentiation:** Provide varying levels of complexity in scenarios

**Technology Integration:** Use collaborative mapping tools for real-time co-creation

### Evaluate
**Duration:** 15 minutes

**Facilitator Actions:** Facilitate peer review of enhanced Wardley Maps and lead a reflection on the learning process.

**Learner Activities:** Present enhanced Wardley Maps, provide peer feedback, and reflect on the applicability to their own professional contexts.

**Resources Used:** Peer review rubric, reflection prompts

**Differentiation:** Offer multiple formats for presenting (verbal, visual, written)

**Technology Integration:** Use digital feedback tools for anonymous peer reviews

## Assessment Methods
* **Formative**: Observation of group discussions and analysis during Explore phase
  - Alignment: Assesses ability to analyze criticisms of Wardley Mapping
* **Summative**: Evaluation of enhanced Wardley Maps created during Elaborate phase
  - Alignment: Assesses ability to create strategies for enhancing Wardley Mapping application

## Differentiation Strategies
* **Novice Wardley Mappers**: Provide additional resources on Wardley Mapping basics, pair with experienced mappers
* **Experienced strategists**: Offer more complex scenarios, encourage them to lead group discussions
